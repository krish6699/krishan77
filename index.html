Welcome to the Singapore Wildlife Adventure!

Enter a world where birds soar and animals roam. Under the Singapore Wildlife Foundation, the Singapore Zoo and Jurong Bird Park have come together as one giant family. Amazing creatures from all across the world can be found here.There's so much to see and learn, from entertaining primates to vibrant parrots. Every visit to one of our parks offers the opportunity to make new acquaintances and discover the amazing lives of the hundreds of species that call them home.

However, it's more than just games and enjoyment. We also take the protection of the priceless species of our world seriously. We're working hard to ensure that animals and their habitats are protected for future generations via research, education, and conservation initiatives.Join in for the crazy journey with us! At the Singapore Wildlife Adventure, there's always something fascinating in store for visitors and locals alike.

Welcome to the Singapore Wildlife Foundation, where passion meets purpose in the pursuit of wildlife conservation and education. Established as a beacon of hope for our planet's biodiversity, we are dedicated to protecting endangered species, preserving natural habitats, and inspiring future generations to become stewards of the environment. Through innovative research, impactful conservation initiatives, and immersive educational programs, we are committed to making a positive difference for wildlife and the world we share.
